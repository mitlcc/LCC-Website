Cena a las Seis

Cena a las Seis, or "Dinner at Six," is an annual LCC-orgnaized Family Weekend event where we bring MIT students and their families together for a semi-formal evening of Latino cuisine and student performances and presentations. The student groups that annually participate in the organization and excecution ofthis event are the Latino Cultural Center, La Unión Chicana por Aztlán, the MIT Chapter of the Society of Mexican American Engineers and Scientists, the Association of Puerto Rican Students, and the MIT Chapter of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers.

This year, Cena a las Seis 2011 will be held on Saturday, October 15th at 6PM in Morss Hall of Walker Memorial.

Please check back at the beginning of the fall semester for more details on speakers and performers for the event. We hope to see you there!

¡Gala Sabrosura!

¡Gala Sabrosura!, the semi-formal Latino cultural night, is held during the spring semester. In the past, the event has been very successful, attracting attendances of over 150 guests. This year, we hope to draw an even bigger and more diverse crowd by organizing a more aggressive advertising campaign and contracting a professional DJ to play only the best Latino music throughout the night.

Please check back at the beginning of the spring semester for more details regarding ¡Gala Sabrosura 2012!, including scheduled performers.

Mes Latino

The purpose of Mes Latino, or Latino Heritage Month, is to unite Latinos on MIT’s campus and promote Latino culture to the entire MIT community. Over the years, Mes Latino has organized traditional as well as innovative events to pull students, faculty and staff together in celebrating Hispanic culture. This year, our main goal is to invite those from outside of the Latino community to join us in celebrating and learning about our culture.

Please check our Calendar page for more details about upcoming events.
